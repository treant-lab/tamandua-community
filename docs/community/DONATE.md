# Donate / Support Tamandua

Tamandua is an OSS-led, self-funded project. Donations are entirely optional and
**no feature, fix, or support is ever gated behind a payment**. Contributions of
code, detection rules, benchmarks, and bug reports are valued just as much as
financial support.

## What donations fund

- Hardware for the test lab (Proxmox hosts, ARM boards, signer-class workstations).
- Code-signing certificates and notarization (Windows Authenticode, Apple Developer Program).
- Infrastructure for CI matrices and benchmark runs.
- Continued maintenance and development time.

## Other ways to support

- Star and share the repositories.
- Contribute detection rules, probes, or benchmark runs (see [CONTRIBUTION_TRACKS.md](./CONTRIBUTION_TRACKS.md)).
- Report bugs and security issues responsibly (see the root `SECURITY.md`).
- Run Tamandua in your own lab and share honest results.
