## Why This Matters

Detection rules need more than a match condition. High-quality rules should include:

- title;
- severity;
- MITRE ATT&CK mapping where applicable;
- required telemetry;
- false-positive notes;
- safe validation notes;
- owner or review path.

## Public Claim Boundary
